Hamidia Road Area

- Hamidia Road is a major populated area of Bhopal city which stretches for a length of more than 1 Kms in the city Bhopal in subdistrict Huzur of the district Bhopal. It connects peer gate area, railway colony areas.
- It has a variety of stores for daily requirements and is also safe for women with many government buildings around and also retail shops for sudden needs of sanitary pads or anything else.
